Our Users & Their Goals

This website serves surfers and oceanography enthusiasts, aiming to enhance their understanding of wave patterns in Morro Bay.

The Task at Hand

Our primary task is to clarify the factors that affect breaking wave sizes in Morro Bay. Through detailed analysis, we enable better planning and deeper understanding for our users.

Data Utilized

Our research is supported by multiyear hourly data from Surfline, providing a solid foundation for analyzing long-term trends and seasonal variations in wave heights.

Visualization Techniques & Tools

Explore the cutting-edge tools and methods we use to bring complex data to life, making it accessible and actionable for our audience.

Interactive Charts

We use dynamic, interactive charts to represent changes in wave heights over time, allowing users to visualize trends at a glance and explore details as needed. Our visualizations are crafted using Altair, a powerful statistical visualization library for Python.

Geospatial Mapping

Our use of geospatial mapping tools, such as Mapbox, enhances understanding by situating data within the geographic layout of Morro Bay, providing a spatial context to the wave data.

Customizable Data Aggregation

Our application provides users with the ability to explore each variable and aggregate the data based on date, time, and selected variables, offering a tailored analytical experience and deeper insights into the data.

Flask Web Application

Our project is supported by a custom Flask web application, which serves as the interactive platform for our visualizations, facilitating real-time data interaction and exploration.
